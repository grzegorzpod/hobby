My offer:

If you need any unique electronic hardware or software, I can design and develop it for you. You can order from me design and development both electronic hardware or software:

Electronic devices

  • Digital thermometers, higromethers and other phisical properties meters,
  • Analog / digital converters, data recorders, oscilloscopes, data aquisition systems,
  • Controllers on / off, regulated power A/C and D/C,
  • Programmers and readers,
  • Communication thru: RS232C, LPT, USB, I2C/TWI, 1-WIRE, Bluetooth LE, Ethernet, WIFI

Software development

  • Desktop apps for DOS - languages: QBasic, Turbo Pascal
  • Desktop apps for Windows 9X/XP/7/10 - languages: Delphi, Visual Basic i C# .NET Framework
  • Apps for devices with Windows CE - language: Visual Studio
  • Apps for devices with microcontrollers ATTiny/ATMega/ESP32: C, Arduino, Bascom AVR, Micropython
  • Apps for devices with linux: C, Bash, Python
  • My apps are easy, powersafe, stable, ready for work 24/7.
